An Automated Positioning Device

Purpose:

This patent describes an automated positioning device designed to achieve precise alignment of interface modules in multiple directions (front, back, left, right, up, and down). It aims to enhance the stability and accuracy of positioning for various applications, particularly in automated systems.

Principle:

The device includes several key components:

Positioning Rod Module:

  • Consists of a positioning rod, an external setup module, and elastic switch buttons arranged in the front, back, left, right, up, and down positions around the rod.
  • The external setup module is fixedly connected to an interface module.

Fixed Column Module:

  • Includes a lower column fixed at the bottom, a middle column connected to the lower column via a rotating shaft, and an upper column connected to the middle column with bolts, allowing vertical movement.
  • The upper column is connected to the positioning rod module.

Movement Modules:

  • Left-Right Movement Module: Contains a transmission mechanism and a support platform for lateral movement.
  • Up-Down Movement Module: Contains a transmission mechanism and a support platform for vertical movement.
  • Front-Back Movement Module: Contains a transmission mechanism and a support platform for front-back movement.

Functionality:

The positioning rod, equipped with elastic switch buttons, detects positional shifts and activates the corresponding movement modules to adjust the position of the interface module. The device ensures high precision and stability in positioning, making it suitable for various automated operations.

Advantages:

High Accuracy and Stability:

Provides accurate positioning in all directions, enhancing the precision and reliability of automated processes.

Efficient and Flexible Operation:

The automated control and modular design allow for quick and flexible adjustments, saving time and improving efficiency.

Scalability and Adaptability:

Suitable for a wide range of applications, including industrial automation, transportation, and machinery, supporting the development of large-scale and intelligent systems.
