A High-Frequency Oscillating Charging System Controlled by PLC

Purpose:

This patent introduces a high-frequency oscillating direct current (DC) charging system controlled by a Programmable Logic Controller (PLC). The system aims to improve the efficiency and speed of charging batteries, particularly for electric vehicles, by using high-frequency pulse modulation.

Principle:

The system comprises several key components:

High-Frequency Pulse Control Output Module:

  • Components: PS switch power supply, PLC programmable controller, pulse boost transformer.
  • Function: Generates high-frequency pulse DC signals (20,000 Hz, 0-5V) which are then amplified to 0-30V by the pulse boost transformer.

Three-Phase Transformer:

  • Components: Input coil and two output coils.
  • Function: Connects to a 380V AC power supply to form a current loop and assists in boosting the voltage for the charging process.

High-Frequency Pulse Charging Module:

  • Components: Two pulse DC oscillating charging circuits, each including a three-phase transformer output coil, a battery pack, and a silicon-controlled rectifier (SCR).
  • Function: Converts the amplified high-frequency pulse signals into intermittent, sawtooth-shaped high-voltage DC pulses (0-1000V) to charge the battery pack efficiently.

Silicon-Controlled Rectifier (SCR):

  • Components: Anode (A), Cathode (K), Control Gate (G).
  • Function: Controls the flow of high-frequency pulse signals through the charging circuit, synchronized by the PLC and the pulse boost transformer.

Advantages:

Increased Charging Efficiency:

Utilizes high-frequency pulses to enhance the battery's ability to accept charge, breaking the limitations of conventional charging methods.

Rapid Charging:

The system's ability to generate intermittent high-voltage pulses allows for quicker charging cycles, reducing overall charging time.

Enhanced Battery Life:

The oscillating pulses reduce internal pressure and gas generation within the battery, improving its longevity and performance.

Flexible and Adjustable:

The PLC allows for precise control of pulse amplitude, frequency, and duty cycle, adapting the charging process to different battery types and conditions.

Key Features:

  • PLC Programming: The controller manages the generation and timing of high-frequency pulses, ensuring optimal charging conditions.
  • Pulse Boost Transformer: Amplifies the initial low-voltage pulses to high-voltage pulses suitable for efficient battery charging.
  • Synchronization Circuit: Ensures the SCRs operate in sync with the high-frequency pulses, maintaining stable and effective charging.

Detailed Operation:

The system begins by converting 220V AC to 24V DC using the PS switch power supply to power the PLC.

The PLC generates high-frequency pulse signals, which are then amplified by the pulse boost transformer.

These amplified pulses are used to control the SCRs, which in turn regulate the flow of high-voltage pulses to the battery pack.

The oscillating nature of the pulses allows for intermittent charging, giving the battery time to absorb the charge effectively and reducing gas formation.

This high-frequency oscillating charging system represents an advanced solution for fast and efficient battery charging, particularly suited for electric vehicles, where reducing downtime and enhancing battery life are critical.
