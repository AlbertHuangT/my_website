An Electric Vehicle Battery Module for Quick Replacement

Purpose:

This patent describes an electric vehicle (EV) battery module designed to facilitate the rapid replacement of batteries. It aims to overcome the challenges of battery size differences, lack of real-time monitoring, and cumbersome manual battery replacement.

Principle:

The battery module includes standardized components and automated systems to streamline the battery replacement process. Key components and their functions are as follows:

Standardized Battery Housing:

  • Fixed relative to the front axle of the EV.
  • Ensures consistent positioning for easy battery replacement.

Battery Components:

  • Battery Body: Includes left and right side protective covers.
  • Communication Module: Features an identification chip and signal transmitter for real-time monitoring and communication.
  • Monitoring and Alarm Module: Monitors battery status and triggers alarms if battery levels drop below a threshold.

Automatic Door and Battery Fixation:

  • Automatic Door System: Operated by a stepper motor controlled from the driver's cabin to open and close the battery compartment door.
  • Battery Fixation System: Uses automatic screws and motor-driven pulleys to secure the battery in place, ensuring stability during operation.

Battery Transfer System:

  • Includes multiple transmission rollers and a telescopic coupling that connects the internal battery shaft to external power sources.
  • Allows for smooth insertion and removal of the battery from the housing.

Advantages:

Quick and Automated Battery Replacement:

The system's automation minimizes manual intervention, reducing the time required to replace batteries.

Enhanced Monitoring and Communication:

Real-time monitoring and communication capabilities enable proactive management of battery health and efficient scheduling of replacements.

Universal Design:

Standardized components ensure compatibility across different EV models, making the system versatile and scalable for widespread adoption.

Improved Safety and Efficiency:

Automated mechanisms and secure fixation enhance safety during battery changes and ensure reliable operation of the EV.
