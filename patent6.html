A Rotating Charging Platform for Quick Battery Replacement in Electric Vehicles

Purpose:

This patent introduces a rotating charging platform designed for the quick swapping and charging of electric vehicle (EV) batteries. It aims to address the inefficiencies in current EV charging and battery replacement systems.

Principle:

The platform consists of several key components:

Rotating Charging Turntable:

  • Includes inner and outer circular tracks with multiple battery charging positions arranged at intervals.
  • Each charging position has transmission rollers and a transmission shaft for battery movement.

Turntable Walking Module:

  • Located on the right side of the turntable, it drives the step-by-step rotation of the charging platform.

Battery Charging Module:

  • Simultaneously charges multiple batteries placed in the charging positions.

Central Fixed Platform:

  • Located inside the left end of the turntable, it houses the walking module and battery exchange controller.

Walking Module:

  • Includes a transmission motor, transmission shaft, gears, and a retractable coupling.
  • Coordinates with the battery exchange controller to manage battery movement on the turntable.

Battery Exchange Controller:

  • Positioned on a conveyor belt, it ensures smooth battery transfer.
  • Facilitates the movement of charged batteries off the turntable and uncharged batteries onto it.

Advantages:

Simultaneous Charging and Replacement:

The turntable structure allows multiple batteries to be charged simultaneously and replaced sequentially based on their charging status.

Automated and Efficient Operation:

The integration of automated systems enhances the efficiency of battery replacement, reducing the need for manual intervention.

High Capacity and Scalability:

Capable of handling a large number of batteries, making it suitable for large-scale EV deployments.

Integrated Monitoring and Control:

The system includes modules for real-time monitoring of battery status, ensuring effective management and timely replacement.

Streamlined Design:

The compact and organized structure facilitates easy operation and maintenance, improving overall efficiency.

Detailed Components:

  • Battery Charging Positions: Each position includes rollers and gears for moving and positioning batteries.
  • Transmission Mechanisms: Ensure precise movement and alignment of batteries during the charging and replacement processes.
  • Retractable Coupling: Connects the transmission shaft to the battery positions, enabling smooth transfer and secure fixation.
  • Infrared Sensors: Used for detecting battery positions and coordinating the movement of the exchange controller.
  • Power Supply System: Includes power poles and cables for delivering electricity to the batteries, equipped with safety features to prevent overcharging.

This system is designed to meet the growing demand for efficient and large-scale EV battery management, providing a practical solution for rapid battery replacement and simultaneous charging.
