An Automated Lifting Electric Vehicle Battery Mounting System

Purpose:

This patent details an automated lifting system for mounting and exchanging electric vehicle (EV) batteries. The system aims to streamline the battery swapping process, making it more efficient and user-friendly.

Principle:

The system consists of several key components designed to automate the lifting, lowering, and transferring of EV batteries:

Trapezoidal Battery Compartment:

  • Located at the bottom of the EV, it matches the shape of the battery and facilitates secure placement.

Battery Mounting Columns:

  • Front and Rear Mounting Columns: These columns are connected to the automatic lifting systems and support the battery frame (basket).
  • Battery Basket: A frame that holds the battery, equipped with left and right frames to support the battery ends.

Automatic Lifting Systems:

  • Front and Rear Lifting Systems: Each includes a drive motor, drive screw, and lifting nut to raise and lower the mounting columns and battery basket.

Battery Transfer Mechanism:

  • Vehicle Passing Module: A grid structure that allows the EV to pass over while also housing a battery transfer system.
  • Transmission Rollers: Embedded in the grid structure, these rollers facilitate the movement of the battery to and from the charging platform.

Battery Positioning and Connection:

  • Left Positioning Baffle: Aligns the battery with the power supply and charging connectors.
  • Positive and Negative Connection Posts: Ensure secure electrical connections for charging and power supply.

Advantages:

Automated Battery Exchange:

Reduces manual intervention, speeding up the battery swapping process.

Precise Positioning:

Ensures accurate alignment and secure placement of the battery using positioning cones and slots.

Efficient Charging Integration:

The system seamlessly transfers batteries to the charging platform and back to the vehicle once charged.

Enhanced Safety and Reliability:

Automated lifting and positioning reduce the risk of mishandling and ensure consistent operation.

Detailed Operation:

Battery Removal:

The EV drives into the battery exchange station, aligning with the front tire limit slot.

The driver presses the battery exchange confirmation button, triggering the lowering of the vehicle passing module and exposing the transmission rollers.

The automatic lifting systems lower the battery basket, placing the battery onto the transmission rollers.

The battery is then transferred to the charging platform.

Battery Installation:

A fully charged battery is transferred from the charging platform to the EV’s battery compartment via the transmission rollers.

The automatic lifting systems raise the battery basket, engaging the positioning cones with the battery's positioning slots, ensuring a secure fit.

The system confirms the battery is in place and locks it securely.

The vehicle passing module is raised back to its initial position, allowing the EV to drive away.

This automated system enhances the efficiency and reliability of battery exchanges in electric vehicles, supporting faster turnaround times and reducing downtime.
