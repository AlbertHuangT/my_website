Charging of Electric Vehicles

Purpose:

This patent introduces an automated platform designed for the rapid swapping and charging of electric vehicle (EV) batteries, facilitating efficient battery management for large-scale use.

Principle:

The platform includes several components:

  • Battery Swapping Position: Equipped with a cross-shaped lifting support platform and a positioning module to ensure precise alignment of the battery housing.
  • Battery Module: Standardized battery housing with automated door and securing mechanisms, allowing smooth battery exchange.
  • Automated Positioning Module: Utilizes rods and switches to align the battery housing with the vehicle’s chassis in multiple directions.
  • Conveyor Modules: Automated conveyors transport batteries between the vehicle and the charging platform.
  • Rotating Charging Turntable: Simultaneously charges multiple batteries and manages their sequence for efficient replacement.
  • Control Module: Operates all motors and conveyors, ensuring seamless battery transfer and charging operations.

The system allows for the quick and automated exchange of EV batteries, improving efficiency and scalability for widespread deployment.
